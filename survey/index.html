The Data

Data Visualisation Census 2013:

I spent some time cleaning up the data from the survey, building on the work of Paje, Gonzolez and Kirk. The @ prefix has been removed from the twitter name, and the text is all lowercase. Duplicates entries and invalid names have been removed. The date of interest field has been standardised and split into months and years.

The location field has been split into town and country where available. The country names have been standardised to match the names used in the world-county-names.tsv file. Finally the latitude and longitude coordinates have been completed using www.latlong.net

The revised data is used below and the csv is available directly.
