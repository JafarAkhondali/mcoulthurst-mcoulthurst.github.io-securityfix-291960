Office for National Statistics

Sample front page summary graphic

The Office for National Statistics publishes a massive amount of data every single day. This includes various economic summaries, bulletins and reports ("releases") that are published at regular intervals throughout the year. For this piece I examined an existing monthly bulletin and presented the information in a visual way. The idea was to build a template that generated the infographic automaticaly based on data from a given bulletin. This graphic was produced by hand, using Illustrator. The actual bulletin (Retail Sales December 2012) can be found on the ONS website.
