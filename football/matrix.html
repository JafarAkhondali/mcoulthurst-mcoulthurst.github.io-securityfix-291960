Bolton Wanderers vs. Manchester City

Saturday 21st August 2011

Inspired by Brendan Dawes' Cinema Redux™ project, this experiment takes the passing data from Bolton vs Man City and maps the actions and events from the match. Actual passes, possession, shots and goals are all mapped, second by second to produce a unique visual fingerprint.

The data is from a number of sources with the main coordinates used with thanks to Opta
